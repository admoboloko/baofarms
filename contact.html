Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common inquiries

How can I purchase your products?

You can purchase our products directly from our farm shop, through our online store, or from our partner retailers. For bulk orders, please contact us directly via phone or email to discuss pricing and delivery options.

Do you offer farm tours?

Yes, we offer scheduled farm tours for educational purposes and for those interested in learning about our sustainable farming practices. Tours must be booked in advance through our contact form or by calling our office.

How long does it take to get a response to inquiries?

We typically respond to all inquiries within 24-48 business hours. For urgent matters, we recommend calling our office directly for immediate assistance.

What areas do you deliver to?

We currently deliver to most areas within Lagos and surrounding regions. For locations outside our standard delivery zone, please contact us to discuss special delivery arrangements.
