About Us

Learn more about Bao Farms and our commitment to sustainable agriculture

Our Story

Founded in 2015 by Blessing Anapuwa Oputa, Bao Farms began as a small poultry farm with just 50 chickens. With a passion for agriculture and sustainable farming practices, Blessing set out to create a farm that would not only provide quality products but also respect the environment and support the local community.

Over the years, Bao Farms has grown into a diversified agricultural enterprise, expanding into fishery, crop production, and farm consulting services. Today, we are proud to be one of the leading agricultural businesses in the region, known for our commitment to quality and sustainability.

Our journey has been marked by continuous learning, innovation, and a deep respect for traditional farming wisdom. We believe that the future of agriculture lies in finding the perfect balance between modern techniques and time-tested practices.

Our Mission

To produce high-quality, sustainable agricultural products while promoting environmental stewardship and supporting local communities through responsible farming practices.

Our Vision

To be the leading sustainable agricultural enterprise in Nigeria, setting standards for quality, innovation, and environmental responsibility in the farming industry.

Our Values

  • Quality Excellence
  • Environmental Stewardship
  • Community Support
  • Innovation & Learning
  • Integrity & Transparency
